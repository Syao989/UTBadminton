What's Badminton Like?

We are UT Badminton, the official UT club for the sport of badminton. We meet at Gregory Gymnasium (GRE) on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7:30PM to 10:00PM and on Sundays from 4:00PM to 7:30PM.

We accept members from all skill levels, so don't hesitate to come try us out! All UT affiliated members are eligible to join. Our policy is to give new members a two-time trial session to see if UT Badminton is the right club for you. After that, we ask that all members pay the club dues necessary to keep our organization running.
